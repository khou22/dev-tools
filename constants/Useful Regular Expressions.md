# Useful Regular Expressions

### **All regexes are in Javascript's `RegExp` type**

1. JPG or PNG file check: `/.+\.(jpe?g|png)
2. Email validity: `/^(([^<>()\[\]\\.,;:\s@"]+(\.[^<>()\[\]\\.,;:\s@"]+)*)|(".+"))@((\[[0-9]{1,3}\.[0-9]{1,3}\.[0-9]{1,3}\.[0-9]{1,3}])|(([a-zA-Z\-0-9]+\.)+[a-zA-Z]{2,}))$/`
